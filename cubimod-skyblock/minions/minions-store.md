# Minions Store

To access the minion store, type **/minions store**. You can purchase various types of minions, including:

* Miner Minion
* Collector Minion
* Feeder Minion
* Fisher Minion
* Farmer Minion
* Lumberjack Minion
* Slayer Minion
* Seller Minion

Each minion has a specific task to help automate resource collection on your Skyblock island.
